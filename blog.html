Jiza Agashe

Certified Financial Planner (CFPCM)

MBA (Finance)

RIA - INA 000007447 SEBI Registered Investment Advisor

Ms. Jiza Agashe, a first generation woman entrepreneur; started her career working for the airline industry. Her understanding of individual customer needs & preferences, and her insight into the B2C businesses, began in the year 1991.

Later Jiza decided to take a break & took on the responsibilities of a homemaker. In 2009 Jiza decided to explore opportunities in the Financial Advisory Services Sector. She graduated as a Certified Financial Planner in 2012. Jiza is now a Registered Investment advisor with SEBI.

Since 2013 Jiza has been in the field of Financial Advisory services. The experience and insight she gained during these years, motivated her to explore ways n means of serving customers, by designing & building business models that would not only serve their general needs but equally provide them access to Domain Experts in the field. Today while its normal for customer’s, to opt for free advice – {a synonym- for anything one can access on the internet} she was convinced – there are millions of customers who value their time & are willing to pay- for advisory services- that ensures one’s comfort & security -in terms of financial well-being.
