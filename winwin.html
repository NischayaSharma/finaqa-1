Win Win Situation

FinaQA™ is the perfect plaform for individuals and businesses for financial planning and resolving legal queries. Many of you would be wondering, “Why is this is a win-win situation?” Well, it is a win-win situa:on for you (the user) and the consultant (professional). In summary, the user gets their financial queries resolved by an expert in the respective financial sector, and the consultant gets a plaform to increase their clientele and need not rely only on referrals.

Advantage for the User :

Unless you have the time & the expertise to sort out your issues concerning your finances; personal financial issues can be challenging and mistakes unknowingly could affect your financial wellbeing. FinaQA™ not only gives you the most suitable (as per your risk profile) solutions to your monetary concerns by experts from different domains, they also have your best interests at heart. Our process of empaneling domain experts works towards ensuring the customer gets the best service. It is also Time saving and affordable. Saves you :me on Internet searches.

Saves you time taken up in searching (read, referrals) for a good consultant, making appointments and also saves travelling costs to the office of the consultant. The answers are provided in the quickest and easiest possible way, given that some queries may require extensive calculations or research on the consultant’s part. It is worth noting that ratio of advisors to clients is very less; it helps to have such an app on the mobile.

Advantage for the Consultant:

FinaQA™ is a ready-made platform to help you access clients. It reduces the need to depend only on referrals. For Consultants particularly in the startup phase, FinaQA™ provides an opportunity to access fee-paying users who could become prospective clients. For the consultants who have established practices, there’s an opportunity to increase the number of fee-paying clients. Economical in terms of setting up infrastructure for your practice. Your mobile and laptop can be your office. Saves time in making client visits. The platform offers you comfort & convenience in terms of managing receipt of your professional fees.

Communication helps build trusts:

Last but not the least, the purpose and aim of FinaQA™ as a Question/Answer platform, is to initiate communication & build TRUST between the User and the Consultant/Advisor.
